ME

My name is Erik Isaksen, I'm a 28 year old guy born and raised in Sandefjord, Norway.

I've always had a huge interest in computers and anything computer related,
and spent countless hours in front of the monitor.
Since 2012 I've worked as a ramp-agent, de/anti-icer and loading supervisor at Torp Airport, Sandefjord.
During these years I've learned to work in team, I've been given leader roles and I have also trained new employees.
Due to the pandemic I got layed off and started searching for new challenges.

Like I mentioned I've always had fun with computer related things, so I decided to teach myself some programming.
I finished Codecademy's course in JavaScript and really enjoyed that. In August I heard about a school called Get Academy.
It's a school that primary focuses on 21st centuary skills together with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
I've learned a lot here, but most importantly I've learned that I really enjoy programming!
